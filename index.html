About us

M/s. S G P G & ASSOCIATES is a Professional CA Firm established by a team of young, enthusiastic, highly skilled and motivated professionals. Our offices are located at Zirakpur (Punjab) & New Delhi. We provide a range of professional services to clients across PAN india basis. The firm is renowned for its ethical approach, attention to detail and providing the client with an honest, objective and balanced opinion.

Working with us has many benefits for you, your business and your stakeholders. We are known for our creative, yet professional approach, which is based on establishing a unique relationship with each of our clients.Over the past years, the CA firm has gained in-depth and deeply experience in providing infinite services to a large number of corporate and non-corporate clients. Our aim to provide the uninterrupted professional services in a cost-effective and in the efficient manner. S G P G & Associates builds a long term and healthy relationship with their clientele.
